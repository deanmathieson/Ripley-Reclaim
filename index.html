Welcome to Ripley Reclaim

About Us

Welcome to ‘Ripley Reclaim’, we started out over 35 years ago restoring old buildings & barn conversions installing oak beams, Log burner radiators etc. We then started doors / floors and other reclaimed items. Now the reclaimed items have developed into a treasure trove of beautiful items from the past.
Even after so many years, we still look forward to the next day’s work and what will turn up at the yard.

Our Specialities

Reclaimed Doors

We have a large stock of sustainable, environmentally friendly reclaimed hardwood doors from cottage doors to large reclaimed church doors, we're sure to have a door in stock to suit your house perfectly and help re-create a period look.

Architectural Salvage

We stock a range of unique products from the Georgian, Victorian and Edwardian eras. These items vary from a single doorknob to an entire stone building, we salvage a variety of materials as well as providing cleaning and restoration services.

Antiques & Bygones

Ripley Reclaim supply to demand, step into Aladdin’s Cave as we stock a variety of weird and wonderful collectables ranging from Victorian Lamposts to World War 2 German Jeeps - whatever your needs, if you want it we’ll get it...

Reclaimed Floors

We carry a stock of approximately 15-20,000 square yards of air-dried, reclaimed hardwood flooring in over 60 varieties supplied in either strip, plank or parquet. Our materials are stacked in cool dry areas to maintain the highest quality.
